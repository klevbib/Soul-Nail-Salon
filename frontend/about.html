About Us

The story behind Soul Nail Salon

Who We Are

Soul Nail Salon was founded with a simple belief: that everyone deserves to feel cared for, confident, and beautiful. Based in the heart of Altrincham, we are a boutique nail salon built on a passion for nail artistry and a genuine love for our community.

From classic manicures to intricate nail art, every service we offer is delivered with precision, creativity, and an eye for detail. We don't just do nails — we create an experience.

Our Story

Soul Nail Salon began as a small studio with a big dream. Our founder, driven by years of experience in the beauty industry and a desire to create a welcoming space for all, opened our doors to bring a higher standard of nail care to Melbourne.

Since then, we have grown into a trusted local destination, known for our skilled technicians, hygienic practices, and warm, personal service. We take pride in building long-lasting relationships with every client who walks through our door.

Our Values

Everything we do is guided by three core values:

  • Quality — We use only premium, professional-grade products and stay up to date with the latest techniques and trends.
  • Care — We genuinely care about the health and wellbeing of your nails, not just how they look.
  • Inclusivity — Our salon is a welcoming space for everyone, regardless of age, background, or style.

Our Team

Our technicians are fully trained, certified, and passionate about what they do. Each member of our team brings their own creative flair and technical expertise, ensuring that whether you're after a simple polish or an elaborate nail design, you're in the best possible hands.

We invest in continuous training so our team is always growing, always learning, and always delivering the best results for you.

Hygiene & Safety

We take hygiene seriously. All tools and equipment are sterilised between every client. Single-use items are disposed of after each service, and our salon is cleaned and maintained to the highest standard. Your health and safety are non-negotiable to us.
